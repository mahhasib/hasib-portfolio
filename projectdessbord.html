Overview

This project aimed to revolutionize the way conscious consumers shop online. The "Eco-Friendly E-commerce" platform provides a curated marketplace for sustainable goods while educating users on the carbon footprint of their purchases through real-time data visualization.

problem The Challenge

Users often feel overwhelmed by "greenwashing" and find it difficult to verify the true sustainability of products. The challenge was to create a transparent, trust-based shopping experience without making the interface feel cluttered or overly academic.

lightbulb The Solution

We implemented a "Sustainability Scorecard" for every product. This utilized a 5-tier rating system based on manufacturing, shipping, and material lifecycle. We also designed a minimal checkout flow that highlights carbon-neutral shipping options by default.
