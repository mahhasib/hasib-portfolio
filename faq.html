Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my design services, workflow, and how we can collaborate on your next big project.

What is your design process?

expand_more

My process is structured into five key phases: Discovery, Research, Wireframing, High-Fidelity Design, and Handoff. I believe in heavy collaboration and iterative feedback to ensure the final product exceeds expectations.

How long does a project take?

expand_more

Timelines vary based on complexity. A landing page might take 1-2 weeks, while a full product design or complex mobile app can take 4-8 weeks. I always provide a detailed timeline after our initial discovery call.

Do you offer revisions?

expand_more

Yes, absolutely. My standard packages include three rounds of major revisions. I aim for total satisfaction, and we'll keep refining until the design perfectly represents your brand and solves your users' needs.

What industries do you work with?

expand_more

I specialize in Fintech, HealthTech, and SaaS solutions. However, the principles of good UX/UI design are universal, and I've successfully delivered projects for e-commerce, education, and lifestyle brands.

What tools do you use for design?

expand_more

Figma is my primary tool for UI design and prototyping. For motion and advanced interactions, I use Principle or Rive. For documentation and handoff, I utilize Storybook and Zeplin if required by the dev team.

Still have questions?

I'm always happy to help! Send me a message and I'll get back to you within 24 hours.
